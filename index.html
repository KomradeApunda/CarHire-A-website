About Our Car Hire

Kondosb Car Rentals is a car hire service in Mombasa, Kenya that provides reliable and affordable rental vehicles to our customers. Our vehicles are serviced regularly and are available in a range of sizes and makes, to suit your needs. We also have a variety of add-ons and packages, to make your rental experience more comfortable and enjoyable.
